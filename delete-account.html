Account Deletion Request

If you want to delete your Digimon Vault account and all associated synchronized data stored in Firestore, you can request the full deletion of your information at any time.

How to request deletion:

Please send an informal email to our support address with the subject line "Digimon Vault - Account Deletion".

Support Email: dev.reazon@gmail.com

What happens next?

  • Upon receiving your request, we will verify your identity.
  • Your Firebase Authentication profile and all connected application data stored in the Firestore database will be permanently deleted within 7 days.
  • Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.
